Voice Diary — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The app requests access to the microphone solely for speech recognition purposes. Audio is processed locally via the Google Speech Recognizer and is never stored by the app.

Recognized text is saved to a local database on the user's device.

2. Third-Party Services

If the user chooses to improve recognized text using AI, that text is sent to Groq AI (groq.com) for processing. This happens only upon explicit user action and is entirely optional.

We do not sell or share user data with any third parties for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the user's device. We have no access to this data and do not store it on any servers.

4. Data Deletion

Users can delete all data by uninstalling the app. The local database is removed along with the app.

5. Children's Rights

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age and does not collect data from children.
